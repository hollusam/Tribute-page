Chadwick Boseman

An American actor and play writter

A Picture of Chadwick Boseman

Here's a summary of Chadwick Bosemans life:

  • 1977 - Born in Anderson, South Carolina, U.S
  • 1993 - He started his acting career
  • 1995 - Boseman graduated from T.L. Hanna High School where he played on the basketball team
  • 1995 - He was recruited to play basketball at college but chose the arts instead
  • 1998 - Together with his other classmates, he attended the Oxford Summer Program of the British American Drama Academy at Balliol College, Oxford in England
  • 2000 - He graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in directing from Howard University in Washington D.C
  • 2002 - He worked as the drama instructor in the Schomburg Junior Scholars Program, housed at the Schomburg Center for Research in Black Culture in Harlem
  • 2003 - He was cast in his first television role, an episode of Third Watch
  • 2004 - His best-known play, Deep Azure, was commissioned by the Congo Square Theather Company in Chicago
  • 2006 - It was nominated for Jeff Award for Best New Work
  • 2007 - He directed, wrote and produced the short film Blood Over a Broken Pawn
  • 2008 - Boseman turned Deep Azure into a screenplay and also moved to Los Angeles to pursue his acting career
  • 2013 - Boseman breakthrough role came with the film 42
  • 2014 - He starred in another sporting film, Draft Day
  • 2016 - He was diagnosed with stage III colone cancer
  • 2020 - Dies 28 August, aged 43
